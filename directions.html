Directions


By Subway/Bus

From East Side of Manhattan: Take the 4, 5, or 6 train to 86th Street; or take the M1, M2, M3, or M4 bus along Fifth Avenue (from uptown locations) to 84nd Street.

From West Side of Manhattan: Take the 1, B, or C train to 86th Street, then the M86 crosstown bus across Central Park to Fifth Avenue.

From Penn Station: Take the M4 bus to 84rd Street and Madison Avenue.

See the MTA website for bus and subway maps.

By Car

From The Bronx, Northern New Jersey, and New England: Take southbound Henry Hudson Parkway to 96th Street exit; cross Central Park and turn right on Fifth Avenue.

From Brooklyn and Staten Island: Take Williamsburg Bridge, Manhattan Bridge, Brooklyn Bridge, or Battery Tunnel to northbound/uptown FDR Drive; exit at 96th Street; turn left on York Avenue; turn right on 86th Street; turn left on Fifth Avenue.

From Southern New Jersey: Take New Jersey Turnpike to Holland Tunnel–Uptown exit; northbound Hudson Street becomes Eighth Avenue, which becomes Central Park West; at 86th Street, turn right and cross Central Park; turn right on Fifth Avenue.

From Bronx, Queens, Long Island, Upstate New York, and New England via Triborough Bridge: Take southbound/downtown FDR Drive to 96th Street exit; turn left on York Avenue; turn right on 86th Street; turn left on Fifth Avenue.

From Queens and Long Island via Queensborough Bridge: Use either level; take right-lane exit onto 60th Street (westbound); at Madison Avenue, turn right and drive north/uptown; turn left on 81st Street then left on Fifth Avenue.
